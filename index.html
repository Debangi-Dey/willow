About Us

Willow is the heart of every vintage dish. From food in a cone to Marshmellow Salads. This place gives you a taste of something new, something unique, something memorable. All that was lost will be found, and all that was found will be kept.

Owner's Intro

The owner of this restaurent, also the creator of this website is Debangi Dey. Debangi Dey is a small Indian girl, passionate about food. She dreams of world peace and hopes that one day the whole world will be together and aprecite and love every little difference between them. Trying to ignite her dream, she has crated this restaurent, combining dishes from all over the world and all over the years. She presents Willow with love to all the people of sweet Earth.
